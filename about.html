Shirin Ezzatpourghadim
Visual Designer

Shirin Ezzatpourghadim is a visual designer who lives in Boston. She was born in Iran where she studied her first major Biomedical engineering. In 2014 she moved to the United States with her family and as she always had the passion for art, she decided to follow her dreams and started to study Visual Design. At the same time, she started to learn photography. Shirin left her country, choosing to pursue her dream of being an artist. She is now an expert in Photography and has strong knowledge of design. She is a natural light photographer with an artistic style and a love for candid captures. She is specialized in portrait photography. Beside the love of photography, she has a passion for web development. She started learning web development. She believes that she can put all her work experiences together and become a creative web designer.
